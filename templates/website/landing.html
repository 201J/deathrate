WELCOME TO ZEDMORTRATE

This system helps with the counting of the number
of people who died in Zambia.

ZedMORTRATE is dedicated to providing up-to-date statistics and records on the mortality rate in Zambia. By compiling data from various sources, we aim to offer a clear picture of the number of deaths occurring in the country. This platform is designed for researchers, policymakers, and the general public to access this critical information easily.
Our goal is to ensure that data regarding deaths in Zambia is readily available and accessible for anyone who needs it. We believe that accurate and timely mortality data is essential for public health planning, education, and awareness.
Explore our system to find comprehensive reports, detailed analytics, and much more about mortality rates in Zambia.
