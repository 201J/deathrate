Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

When a loved one passes away, contact the authorities if it happens at home. You will need to contact a medical professional to obtain a death certificate. In some cases, a postmortem may be required, which will be handled by the medical examiner or coroner.

To obtain a death certificate in Zambia, you need to visit the local authority’s office with the required documents, including the deceased's ID and a death notification from a healthcare provider. The certificate is usually processed within a few days.

After obtaining the death certificate, you can contact a funeral home to discuss arrangements. In Zambia, both burial and cremation are accepted, and the choice depends on the family's wishes. The funeral home will assist in coordinating the burial or cremation process.
